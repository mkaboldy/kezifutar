About this app

This app has been built to demonstrate the architecture of an Angular 2 browser app. The mission of the app is to provide departure information for public transport services in Budapest metropolitan area.

The full source code of the app is available on github: https://github.com/mkaboldy/bkk_departures. Feel free to clone, fork or report issues.
